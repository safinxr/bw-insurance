Frequently asked questions

Is The GO Insurance an insurance carrier?

No, we're an insurance comparison site. The big difference is that we compare the top insurance companies in one place to help you find the best policy for you.

How does The GO Insurance work?

All it takes is five minutes. You answer some questions, we'll crunch the numbers to show you quotes from every major insurance company. After you decide on a policy, you can buy online or through one of our licensed insurance agents.

Is The GO Insurance licensed?

Each of our independent insurance agents is licensed to offer policies from every major provider. As your needs change, they'll always be there to help you find the coverage you need.

How does The GO Insurance make money?

Great question. We make money in a couple ways. If you purchase a policy through one of our insurance agents, we earn a commission. When you choose to get a quote, we'll let your chosen insurance company know you're interested. Instead of a thank you card, they send a commission. These commissions allow us to provide our services free of charge to policy shoppers everywhere.

Who am I buying my policy from?

It's your call. If you want to purchase a policy directly through your provider, they'll have your quote ready. Or you could skip the trip to another site and buy a policy through The GO Insurance. No matter which you choose, it's the same policy.

Is my personal primaryrmation secure?

Yes. As real people with real data, we understand the importance of keeping your personal primaryrmation private. That's why we pledge to never sell your data to spammers.
